## What Is Volatility-Based Rebalancing?

Volatility-based rebalancing **adjusts weights according to changes in asset volatility**:

- High volatility → reduce allocation  
- Low volatility → increase allocation  

> Builds on: **[Crypto Portfolio Risk Management Explained (Protect Capital First)](https://txchyon.com/categories/portfolio-management/)**

---

## How to Implement

1. Calculate asset volatility (historical or expected)  
2. Determine target portfolio risk  
3. Adjust allocations inversely to volatility  
4. Rebalance periodically  
5. Track results: **[How to Track Crypto Portfolio Performance (Metrics That Actually Matter)](https://txchyon.com/categories/portfolio-management/)**

---

## Benefits

- Keeps portfolio risk stable  
- Prevents large drawdowns  
- Complements dynamic and factor-based allocation strategies

---

## Final Thoughts

Volatility-based rebalancing is a **data-driven approach** that ensures consistent portfolio exposure during turbulent markets.
